ISRP Official Rules

Below are the community rules. These are subject to change — stay updated to avoid any violations.

Random Death Match (RDM)

Defined as harming another user without a valid RP reason.
(Ex: Hitman RP’s, Drive-bys)

Vehicular Death Match (VDM)

Deliberately disabling someone’s vehicle without RP reason.
(Ex: Spiking cars randomly, body blocking cars)

Failure to Roleplay (FRP)

Doing actions that defy logical RP consequences.
(Ex: Using gun after being cuffed)
