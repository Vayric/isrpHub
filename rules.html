ISRP Official Rules

Below are the community rules. These are subject to change — stay updated to avoid any violations.

Random Death Match (RDM)

Deliberately harming others without valid RP reasoning.
Examples: Hitman RP’s, Assassination RP’s, Drive-bys.

Vehicular Death Match (VDM)

Using vehicles to interfere or crash without RP reasoning.
Examples: Spiking cars, blocking traffic, jumping on hoods.
